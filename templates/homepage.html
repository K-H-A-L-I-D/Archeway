Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Archeway platform.

Is Archeway free to use?

Yes! Archeway offers a comprehensive free plan that includes all core features for tracking and finding internships. We may offer premium plans with additional features for power users in the future, so stay tuned!

How does the job finder work?

Our job finder aggregates internship opportunities from multiple sources and uses your profile information, skills, and preferences to match you with relevant positions.

Can I use Archeway for full-time job hunting?

While Archeway is optimized for internship tracking, many of our users successfully use the platform for entry-level job hunting as well.

Can I export my application data?

Yes, you can export your application data as CSV or PDF files at any time, making it easy to share your progress with career advisors or keep backups.
