About Archeway

The story behind our mission to transform how students find and manage internship opportunities.

What is "Archeway"?

Pronounced: AR-ch-EE-way

Our name has deep meaning. "Arche" comes from Greek, meaning "beginning" or "origin."

"Archeway" represents the beginning of your path—your way forward.

Just as you walk through an arch to enter a new space, Archeway is your gateway to starting your career journey.

How Archeway Came to Life

1

The Problem We Saw

As students ourselves, we experienced firsthand the challenges of managing multiple internship applications across different platforms. Spreadsheets became unwieldy, and valuable opportunities disappeared through the cracks as our systems became overwhelmed.

3

Building the Solution

Our team of three passionate developers came together to build the first prototype of Archeway. After presenting to our peers with great results, we committed to turning our project into a fully-fledged platform that could help students everywhere.

2

The Vision

We envisioned a platform where students could not only keep track of their applications but also discover opportunities tailored to their skills and interests. A single gateway—or archway—to their professional future.

4

Launch and Growth

Archeway launched in beta to our university community in early 2025, and we hope to grow enough to serve students across multiple campuses. Our commitment to student success drives everything we do.

Our Mission

At Archeway, we believe every student deserves access to career opportunities that align with their passions and potential. We're on a mission to democratize the internship search process and help students build meaningful careers from day one.

Accessibility

Making career resources and opportunities accessible to all students regardless of background or institution.

Transparency

Providing clear, honest information about opportunities and the application process to empower informed decisions.

Community

Building a supportive network where students can share experiences, advice, and encouragement during their career journeys.
